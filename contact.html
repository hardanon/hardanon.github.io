PGP Instructions

To send a secure message:

  1. Download and install a PGP-compatible email client or tool.
  2. Import my public PGP key from the link above.
  3. Compose your message and encrypt it using my public key.
  4. Copy the encrypted message into the "Message" field above.
  5. Fill in your email, and subject, then click "Send Email".
