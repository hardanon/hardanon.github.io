Hardpad T400

The Hardpad T400 is equipped with an Intel Core Duo P8400, 4GB of RAM, and a 120GB SSD. It can run either Qubes OS or any other Linux distribution of your choice. Additionally, I can sell you a verified 8GB USB stick with Tails OS for 10 euros, depending on your use case. The device also has an Atheros wifi card installed, offering download and upload speeds of up to 100MB/s. Finally, it has Libreboot installed to replace the stock firmware. Libreboot disables entirely intel ME, an hardware spyware that can be remotely controlled.
